Aspect of the Beast: Choose a animal.
Bear: You gain the might of a bear. Your carrying capacity(including maximum loadand maximum lift) is
doubled, and you have advantages on Strength checks made to push, pull, lift, or break objects.
Eagal: You gain the eyesight of an eagle. You can see up to 1 mile away with no difficulty, able
to discern even fine details as though looking at something no more than 100 ft away from you.
Additionally, dim light doesn't impose disadvantage on your Wisdom (Perception) checks.
Wolf: You gain the hunting sensibilities of a wolf. You can track other creatures while
traveling at a fast pace, and you can move stealthily while traveling at a normal pace.
